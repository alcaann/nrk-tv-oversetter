# Privacy Policy for NRK TV Oversetter

**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

## Overview

NRK TV Oversetter is a browser extension that translates Norwegian subtitles on NRK TV videos in real-time. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection and Use

### What Data We Collect

1. **User Preferences (Stored Locally)**
   - Target translation language
   - Font size settings
   - Subtitle position preferences
   - Extension enabled/disabled state

2. **Website Content**
   - Subtitle text from NRK TV videos (read in real-time for translation purposes only)

### How We Use Your Data

- **User Preferences**: Stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API to remember your settings between browsing sessions
- **Subtitle Text**: Read from NRK TV pages and processed through Chrome's built-in Translator API for real-time translation. This data is not stored, logged, or transmitted to any external servers.

## Data Storage

All user data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's secure storage mechanism. We do not use remote servers, databases, or cloud storage.

## Data Sharing

We **do not**:
- Sell or transfer your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers or analytics services
- Track your browsing history
- Collect personally identifiable information
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Send data to external servers

The extension only uses Chrome's built-in Translator API, which is provided by your browser and operates according to Google's privacy policies.

## Third-Party Services

The extension uses Chrome's built-in Translator API for translation services. Translation is performed locally on your device after downloading the language model (one-time setup). Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information about Chrome's built-in features.

## Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions:

- **storage**: To save your preferences (language, font size, position) locally on your device
- **scripting**: To inject the translation display on NRK TV pages
- **activeTab**: To check if the current tab is an NRK TV page
- **host permission (*.nrk.no/*)**: To access subtitle elements on NRK TV pages for translation

## Data Retention

- **User Preferences**: Retained locally until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data
- **Subtitle Text**: Not retained; processed in real-time and immediately discarded after translation

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear all stored preferences by uninstalling the extension
- View what permissions the extension uses in Chrome's extension settings
- Disable the extension at any time

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.
